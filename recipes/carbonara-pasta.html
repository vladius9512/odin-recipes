Carbonara Pasta

Description

Italians are really proud of this. And you should be too after you will tase this recipe

Ingredients
  • Pasta
  • Gorgonzolla
  • Bacon
  • Parmezan
  • Sower cream
Steps
  1. Put the pasta into water and let it boil for 15 minutes
  2. Burn the bacon. Let it burn for like 3 minutes
  3. Put the sower cream and the cheese over the bacon
  4. Take out the pastas from the water
  5. Put the pasta over the bacon and cheese sauce, and the dish is ready to be served.
