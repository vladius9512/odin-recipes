Tortilla Wrap

Description

It is simply delicious. Bacon, cheese, wrap. What could you want more?

Ingredients
  • Wrap
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Some garlic sauce(optional)
  • tomatoes
Steps
  1. First prepare the dishes
  2. Cut the tomatoes and cheese
  3. Burn the bacon for 5 mins
  4. Throw the bacon and the other ingredients into the wrap
  5. Burn the wrap for 2 mins on each side
  6. Enjoy!
