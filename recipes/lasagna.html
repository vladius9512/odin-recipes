Lasagna recipe

Description

It is simply delicious. Even if you are vegan or a meat lover, this recipe could satisfy your culinary needs.

Ingredients
  • Flour
  • Mozzarela
  • Meat(can be skipped)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Egg plant
Steps
  1. First prepare the dishes
  2. Cut the vegetables into pieces, round
  3. Throw them into the dish
  4. Let it in the oven for 40 mins at 200 degrees
  5. Enjoy your dish
